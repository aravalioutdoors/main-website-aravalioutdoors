to get out

Camping

Sleep under an open sky

Pitch on a ridge as the light drops, cook over a fire, and wake to fog rolling off the valley. Our camps are small, quiet and left exactly as we found them.

Walk the ancient ridgelines

Follow old shepherd trails through dry forest and granite. Half-day strolls to lung-testing climbs — every route is scouted, graded and led by someone who knows the range by heart.
